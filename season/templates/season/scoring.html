Introduction

Although the official point-scoring of Formula 1, Formula 2, Formula 3, and W Series carries over to Fantasy Formula Academy, there are many more ways to score points. This makes the decision about which drivers you'll choose for your academy a little more difficult than picking those most likely to win!

To have the highest-scoring Fantasy Formula Driver Academy at the end of the season you need to think strategically about strong qualifiers, consistent finishers, fastest laps, and the series your drivers' are racing in.

Lead Driver Bonus

In each race series you can pick two drivers; a lead driver and a second driver. It's very important that you choose your lead driver wisely... they will score you extra points!

  • For Formula 1, your lead driver will score a further 10% at each event (i.e. qualifying or a race).
  • For Formula 2, your lead driver scores a further 25% at each event (i.e. qualifying or a race).
  • For Formula 3 and W Series, your lead driver will score a further 50% at each event (i.e. qualifying or a race).

Rounding - Should your lead driver's points tally finish result in a fractional value, these are all stored for sorting your league position.
